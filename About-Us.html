The Art of Perfect Rice, Perfected by Us

Since 1978, we have been masters in cultivating, processing, and exporting basmati & non basmati rice. GK Rice Mills, have been in the business of rice milling for over four decades, continuously improving the process, while domestic and overseas distribution is handled by Bhaskar International Agri Foods. Our specialty, fragrant Basmati Rice, is aged naturally so that it can take its place on the table of every family with authentic flavors ready to cherished smelling nourishing meals.

At this point, we are not just growing rice, but developing a heritage which our children will proudly muster trust, tradition and taste. We commit to serving first class quality nutriments, while at the same time ensuring that every grain, or in this case, rice yield is perfect.

Connecting Continents Through Premium Rice

People from all over the globe have incorporated our premium Basmati & Non-Basmati rice into their preferred dishes. Reliably found on the vibrant shelves of food stores in Nepal, the USA, and the UAE, our rice is synonymous with trust. From royal pleasures in Saudi Arabia to the splendid cuisine of the United Kingdom and the melting pot of Canada and Australia, our grains give pleasure to all meals.

We have also started making our mark in Europe by serving the food lovers in France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, and Belgium. In the Middle East, our rice is enjoyed in Kuwait, Yemen, and Oman, as well as in Asia from Malaysia to Singapore and beyond. Even in South Africa our rices are a household staple.
